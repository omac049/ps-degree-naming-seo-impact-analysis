Risk Analysis

SEO Challenges & Mitigation

Critical risks identified with recommended mitigation strategies.

‚ö†Ô∏è Search Demand Risk Validated via GSC

GSC Data (2025): "Bachelor" queries generated 100K+ impressions while "professional studies" had only 15 impressions. "BPS degree" returned zero results. This confirms the need for dual-language strategy.

Mitigation: Dual-keyword targeting‚Äîinclude both "Professional Studies" AND traditional degree terms ("bachelor's degree", "online bachelor's") on all program pages.

‚ö†Ô∏è Existing Page Rankings at Risk

Current BA/BS program pages may lose organic rankings during URL/content transition.

Mitigation: Implement proper 301 redirects and canonical tags. Consider preserving URLs while updating content.

‚ö° User Unfamiliarity

Prospective students may not recognize or search for "Professional Studies" terminology.

Mitigation: Create educational content explaining the value proposition and career advantages of BPS degrees.

‚ö° Established Competition

Syracuse, Georgetown, and others already rank for BPS-related keywords.

Mitigation: Differentiate with 90-credit messaging and career-focused positioning unique to UAGC.

SEO Migration Runbook

A practical checklist to reduce volatility if/when URLs, titles, and degree labels change. 3 min read

üéØ
Recommended Path: Option A (Preserve URLs)

Keep existing high-performing URL paths and update content in place. This minimizes redirect overhead and preserves URL equity. Only use Option B (new URLs) if there's a compelling business requirement.

üßæ Phase 0 ‚Äî Discovery

  • Inventory impacted URLs and identify "do not break" pages (highest organic + conversions).
  • Export baselines: GA4 by page; GSC by page/query; crawl/index status.

üõ†Ô∏è Phase 1 ‚Äî Build

  • Implement dual-language naming (spell out + plain language first mention).
  • Ship minimum content set: BPS explainer, comparison page, 90-credit landing.
  • Add FAQ modules where appropriate to support FAQ schema.

üöÄ Phase 2 ‚Äî Launch (Day 0)

ü©∫ Phase 3 ‚Äî Stabilize

  • Monitor 404/soft-404, index coverage, rankings, traffic, and conversion deltas daily (first 7‚Äì10 days), then weekly.
