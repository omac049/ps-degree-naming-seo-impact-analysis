Risk Analysis

SEO Challenges & Mitigation

Critical risks identified with recommended mitigation strategies.

‚ö†Ô∏è Search Demand Risk Needs validation

Traditional "bachelor's degree" / BA / BS language is likely higher-demand than "BPS" terminology. Quantify with keyword tools before making volume claims.

Mitigation: Dual-keyword targeting‚Äîinclude both "Professional Studies" AND traditional degree terms on all program pages.

‚ö†Ô∏è Existing Page Rankings at Risk

Current BA/BS program pages may lose organic rankings during URL/content transition.

Mitigation: Implement proper 301 redirects and canonical tags. Consider preserving URLs while updating content.

‚ö° User Unfamiliarity

Prospective students may not recognize or search for "Professional Studies" terminology.

Mitigation: Create educational content explaining the value proposition and career advantages of BPS degrees.

‚ö° Established Competition

Syracuse, Georgetown, and others already rank for BPS-related keywords.

Mitigation: Differentiate with 90-credit messaging and career-focused positioning unique to UAGC.
